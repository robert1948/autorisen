# LinkedIn Video 01 — Black Box to Proof

**Series:** CapeControl Trust-First Video Rollout
**Objective:** Reframe buyer anxiety from "AI answers" to "evidence-backed workflow proof"
**Length:** 60-90 seconds
**Format:** Founder to camera + UI proof cutaways
**Primary CTA:** See How It Works
**Secondary CTA:** Start Guided Onboarding

## Audience

- Compliance-heavy operators and team leads
- Decision-makers skeptical of unverifiable AI outputs

## Core Message

If you cannot prove where an answer came from, you do not have automation. You have risk. CapeControl is built to show its work.

## Script (Voiceover / On-Camera)

## 0:00-0:07 Hook

"Most AI tools are black boxes. They give you answers, but not proof."

## 0:08-0:20 Problem

"In compliance-heavy teams, that breaks trust fast. The questions always come: where did this number come from, which source was used, can I defend this to an auditor?"

## 0:21-0:42 Contrast

"We built CapeControl differently. Not another chatbot. A governed workflow platform where every output can trace back to approved sources, every critical step can pause for human review, and activity is audit-logged by default."

## 0:43-0:60 Proof

"Here is the difference in practice: source citation visible, decision checkpoint visible, audit event visible. Calm before complexity."

## 0:61-0:75 CTA

"If your team needs answers you can actually defend, see how it works at cape-control.com."

## Shot List

1. Founder talking head (close framing) for hook and problem.
1. Cut to UI: workflow output with source citation panel visible.
1. Cut to UI: approval checkpoint modal or review step.
1. Cut to UI: activity/audit feed entry created after action.
1. Return to founder for CTA.

## Editing Notes

- Hard-cut pacing, no long transitions
- Keep captions burned in
